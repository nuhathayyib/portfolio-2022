Temple Seeds Package Design

Temple seeds is a brand that sells all types of healthy seeds! Seeds are a natural super-food filled with benefits such as boosting peoples health through specific needs of spiritual, mentally and physical health. These packages pack plenty of goodness inside, all to provide a high-quality, healthy snack.
